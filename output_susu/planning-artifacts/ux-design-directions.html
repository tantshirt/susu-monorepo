6. Heritage-first
Diaspora skin (copper/terracotta on warm-tinted ink) as the default, neutral skin opt-in.
Why rejected as default: undermines the "infrastructure neutrality" framing of the protocol. Heritage is voice + opt-in skin, not the default chrome of an audited primitive. However: this becomes the diaspora-skin variant of Direction 1 — same structure, swapped accent tokens, mint stays for protocol identity.

Evaluation criteria applied

Phantom-fintech alignment
Dark-first, generous whitespace, glassy elevation, premium type hierarchy. D1 wins; D3 too cold; D5 too flat.
NOT purple
Andre's explicit veto. Eliminates D4 (gradient includes purple). D1 mint-green is the on-Solana-native pivot.
Dual-skin structural support
Cross-skin invariant tokens (mint for protocol identity) make the dual-skin claim structural. D1+D6 share this.
30-second judge scan
Badge-dense first viewport must read in 30s. D1 + D6 both deliver; D3 too monochrome to differentiate badges.
Accessibility AA minimum
All six clear AA on text. Only D1 (mint on ink) clears AAA on primary buttons; D6 (copper on warm ink) is AA Large.
Distinctive Solana identity
D1 mint owns "on-Solana-native" most decisively without copying Phantom (purple) or Foundation (gradient).
